About Cosmosebike

Explorers
First.

We started with a question: why does a bike with good components cost twice what those components are worth?

The traditional bike industry runs on layers. A manufacturer builds the bike. A national distributor takes margin. A regional importer takes margin. A retail shop takes margin. By the time the bike reaches the rider, you've paid for four businesses, not one product.

"We removed the layers. Not the components."

VAPOR SL runs Shimano 105 Di2 — the same electronic shifting system you'll find on bikes costing twice as much. The same carbon wheelset from Illusionwheels. The same Toray T800 carbon frame. The only thing missing is the markup.

We're based in Germany. The bike is in Germany, at our partner showroom in Mönchengladbach, available to test ride today. We're not an online store that ships mystery boxes from a warehouse. We're a brand with a physical presence, a real address, and a WhatsApp number that a human answers.

"Ride it for 60 minutes. If it's not what we say it is, walk away."

That's the deal. No complex return policies to navigate. No fine print about deposit conditions. If we can't deliver your size, colour, and configuration — you get your money back, in full, without asking twice.
